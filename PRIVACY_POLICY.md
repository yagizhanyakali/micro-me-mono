### Personal Information

When you register for and use our Service, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Authentication Information**: Email address and password (securely stored via Firebase Authentication)
- **Profile Information**: Name or username (if provided)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the Service

### Habit and Activity Data

The core functionality of our Service involves tracking your habits and activities:

- Habit names, descriptions, and categories
- Daily entries and progress tracking
- Statistics and analytics about your habit completion
- Timestamps of activities and entries

### Automatic Information Collection

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages
- **Device Information**: Mobile device ID, hardware model, operating system version
- **Location Data**: General location information (country/region level only)
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: Small data files stored on your device

### Push Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, we collect:
- Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to send you notifications
- Notification preferences and settings
